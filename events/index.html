End of semester LAN party and League of Legends tournament

  • Posted by Cang Truong on November 25, 2014

Association for Computing Machinery and League of Legends Club are joining together to host a Lan Party on Nov. 21st from 6pm to 11pm in SSB 160.

Food will be provided!

Bringing your own games from home is permitted. If you have any extra controllers, feel free to bring those as well!

There will be a League of Legends Tournament held at the LAN as well. First, second, third, and fourth place will be determined at the LAN (Elimination rounds will be played prior to the event). Spectators are welcome!
