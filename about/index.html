Skills and Goals

All About Us

ACM is here to help you reach your academic and professional goals while having fun.

Peer Communication

No matter how much experience you have, there is someone here that can teach you something new. Throughout the semester we have many events where you can meet like-minded peers or learn new things:

  • Hackathons
  • Professionalism Workshops
  • Coffee and Code
  • Member Hosted Workshops

Our Mission

Our goal is to help students be competitive in their post graduate endeavors. There are many critical skills vital to being competitive, and our organization is aimed at helping our members stand out from the crowd. We want to help you develop skills in:

  • Professionalism
  • Interview Preparation
  • Networking
  • Resume Building

Opportunity

Since the University of California at Merced is a relatively new campus, we have the opportunity to make a name for ourselves. In the past we've stormed Hackathons with a full bus of students when the host university did not expect any of our students to apply. There are many opportunities, and we're here to take advantage of them.
