    How do I take notes?
    Simply tap the '+ New note' button. You can swipe left or right to change the note type.
  • How do I delete a note?
    Tap 'Delete' on the bottom right corner of the note page.
    How do I delete all of my notes?
    Tap 'Delete all data' on the settings page.
    Can I retrieve a deleted note?
    No, we do not keep backup versions of your deleted data.
